Discover South Mountain

You've come to the right place to find all the local listings for food, lodging, entertainment, and activities. Discover the best of South Mountain!

Welcome to South Mountain! We are located at the base of South Mountain in the southwest corner of the Salt Lake valley. The South Mountain area is one of Utah's largest growing communities since 2010. Benefits of the area include beautiful homes, safe neighorhoods, and a tight-knit communtiy. Also home to over 2,700 acres of open space, trails, park and our very own Blackridge Resevoir. Explore a wide variety of outdoor activities: hiking, biking, horseback riding, disc golf, pickleball, ice skating, and many more. Enjoy our community events. Shop at many locally owned shops and restaurants. Discover something for everyone to enjoy.
