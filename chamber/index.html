News Release

The South Mountain Chamber of Commerce is hosting it's 5th annual Toy Drive to benefit local children this holiday season. The drive started November 15, and will run through December 15. The Chamber is seeking new and unwrapped gifts, including toys, books, socks, art supplies, ear pods, toiletries, board games, fuzzy blankets, stuffed animals, handheld electronic games, etc. In addition, you can provide a monetary donation and Chamber personnel will be glad to do the shopping for you.
