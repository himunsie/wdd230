Target Audience

Small and local business owners with the South Mountain area

Personas

Person 1

Small business owner, not a chamber member, male, age 25-55

Person 2

Local business owner, chamber member, female, age 25-55

Scenarios

  1. Looking for information on joining the chamber of commerce. How to join?
  2. Wanting to find out what events are happening with local busineses.
  3. Looking for advertising opportunities or perks with the chamber.
  4. Wanting to find other businesses to network with.
  5. Visitor looking for dining, arts, or attractions in the area.
